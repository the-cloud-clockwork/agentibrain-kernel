# Operate

Everything you need from "I have a Kubernetes cluster" to "the kernel is running, healthy, and serving agents."

Start with **Helm Quickstart** for the bare-cluster path. **Deployment** covers ArgoCD + ESO + multi-source patterns once you've outgrown the quickstart. **Secrets**, **Environments**, **Operations**, and **Troubleshooting** are the day-2 references you'll keep open.
